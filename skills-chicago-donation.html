Donors

Invest in Opportunity. Create Pathways to Success.

At Skills for Chicago, we know that access to employment is the foundation of financial stability and stronger futures. Your donation equips job seekers with the tools, support, and employer connections they need to secure long-term, meaningful employment and build brighter futures.

Together, we can create a stronger, more inclusive workforce where job seekers have the resources they need to thrive. Be part of the movement to create pathways to success.

Make A Donation

How Your Donation Makes a Difference

Your gift directly impacts job seekers by funding the services that open doors to employment and financial security.

$50

Interview Prep & Resume Review

Help a job seeker feel more secure and gain confidence through one-on-one professional coaching.

$250

One Hour of Job Seeker Mentoring

Provide personalized career guidance from an industry professional to help job seekers plan their next steps.

$500

Job Readiness Workshop

Fund a career preparation session, equipping job seekers with skills in professional communication, workplace expectations, and networking.

$1000

Community Partner Convening

Support a gathering of community and workforce organizations, and businesses to collaborate on expanding employment access in disadvantaged communities.

$2,500

Place an Individual into a Job

Support the full journey of a job seeker from application to employment. Your investment provides personalized career coaching, job readiness support, and direct employer connections, ensuring a successful placement in a meaningful role.

$5,000

Employer Hiring Barrier Analysis

Invest in an assessment of employer partner hiring practices to identify barriers preventing access to top talent.

Every contribution—big or small—helps create stronger communities, a more inclusive workforce, and long-term job opportunities for job seekers across Chicago.

Ways To Give

Your support can take many forms, and every contribution expands economic opportunity for job seekers in need.

One-Time or Monthly Giving

To stand as a life-changing employment resource by connecting underrepresented job seekers to employers with opportunities across industries.

Employer Match

If your company offers a matching gift program, your donation can double its impact. Simply submit your donation with your employer's matching gift form to maximize your contribution.
