Our Work In Action

Real Impact. Real People. Real Change.

At Skills for Chicago, we are creating pathways to success by expanding access to employment opportunities for underrepresented job seekers. Through firsthand stories and workforce insights, we highlight the success of individuals who have secured meaningful jobs, the latest updates on workforce trends, and the impact of employer and community partnerships. By sharing real experiences and workforce insights, we showcase the impact of equitable employment opportunities and the work being done to remove barriers for job seekers.

Stories of Impact

Every job secured through Skills for Chicago represents more than just employment—it's a step toward financial stability and long-term success. Hear from job seekers who have overcome barriers, found new opportunities, and taken control of their futures.

Welcome to the Energy Workforce Equity Portal

The Portal is an online source designed to help connect clean energy companies with Equity Eligible Persons looking to work in the clean energy sector in Illinois. Making these connections is a key aspect of ensuring that the clean energy industry in Illinois grows in an equitable manner.
