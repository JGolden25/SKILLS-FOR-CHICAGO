Stronger Partnerships.
Greater Impact.

At Skills for Chicago, we believe that meaningful employment starts with strong community connections. By working with nonprofits, workforce organizations, and educational institutions, we expand access to job opportunities, training, and career resources for job seekers across Chicago's south and west sides.

How We Work with Community Partners
  • Connecting Job Seekers to Employment Linking individuals to open roles and hiring employers
  • Partnering with Local Hiring & Networking Events Creating opportunities for job seekers to connect directly with employers
  • Strengthening Local Networks Collaborating to expand career pathways career opportunities and reduce employment barriers
