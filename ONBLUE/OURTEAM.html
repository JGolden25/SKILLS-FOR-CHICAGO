Our Team

Dedicated to Equity. Driven by Impact.

At Skills for Chicago, our team is united by a shared commitment to expanding access to meaningful employment for job seekers across Chicago. With our engaged Board of Directors, we work together to break down barriers to employment, advocate for equitable hiring, and create sustainable economic mobility for underrepresented job seekers across Chicago's south and west sides. Our team is focused on driving employment that goes beyond job placement—building a future where opportunity is accessible to all.

Meet Our Staff

Meet Our Board of Directors

With their expertise, leadership, and commitment to our mission, they help shape policies and partnerships that create lasting impact.
