Partner With Us

Find the Right Talent. Strengthen Workforce.

At Skills for Chicago, we connect businesses with qualified, pre-screened talent they won’t find anywhere else. Our employer partners gain access to job seekers with unique skills and experiences who are ready to contribute and grow. By working with us, companies fill critical roles while driving economic mobility in Chicago.

How We Support Employers

  • Curated Talent Pipelines - Find job-ready candidates aligned with hiring needs.
  • Tailored Recruitment Solutions - Tap into flexible hiring support for various industries.
  • Exclusive Talent Access - Connect with candidates who are not on traditional job boards
  • Hiring Expertise - Access insights to streamline and improve recruitment process.
  • Strategic Workforce Support - Strengthen team with motivated, skilled employees.

Join A Network Of Employers Making A Meaningful Impact in Our City.

Connect with top talent and build a stronger workforce.
