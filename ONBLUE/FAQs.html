FAQs for Job Seekers

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Navigating the job search process can be challenging, but we're here to help. Find answers to common questions about applying for jobs, preparing for interviews, and using our services.

How do I apply for jobs through Skills for Chicago? +

To apply for jobs through Skills for Chicago, you can:

  • Visit our website and browse open positions
  • Create an account and submit resume
  • Attend one of our job fairs or hiring events
  • Contact one of our job coaches for personalized assistance

Our team will review qualifications and connect you with opportunities that match skills and experience.

Do I need to pay for services? +

No, all of our services for job seekers are completely free. We are funded by partnerships with employers and philanthropic organizations to help connect talented Chicago residents with quality employment opportunities at no cost to you.

What industries do you offer jobs in? +

We work with employers across many industries including:

  • Healthcare
  • Manufacturing
  • Hospitality
  • Information Technology
  • Financial Services
  • Transportation & Logistics
  • Retail

Our SUCCESS STORIES continue to grow, expanding the range of opportunities available.

What are the requirements to apply for jobs? +

Basic requirements include:

  • You must be at least 16 years old
  • You must be legally authorized to work in the United States
  • You must be a resident of Chicago or the surrounding area
  • You must complete our registration process

Specific jobs may have additional requirements regarding education, experience, or skills.

How long does the hiring process take? +

The hiring process timeline varies depending on the employer and position. Generally, you can expect:

  • 1-3 days for initial application review
  • 1-2 weeks for interviews to be scheduled
  • 1-3 weeks for final hiring decisions

Our team works to streamline this process as much as possible and keeps you updated at each stage.

Will Skills for Chicago guarantee me a job? +

While we cannot guarantee job placement, we significantly improve chances of finding employment through:

  • Direct connections with hiring employers
  • Personalized resume and interview coaching
  • Job-specific preparation and training
  • Ongoing support throughout the application process

Our success rate is high because we only connect you with positions where skills and experience align with employer needs.

What if I need training or skill development? +

We offer several options for skill development:

  • Free workshops on job-specific skills
  • Connections to training programs and educational resources
  • Online learning opportunities
  • Mentorship and coaching programs

Our team can assess skills and recommend appropriate training paths to help you qualify for target positions.

What if I have a gap in my employment history? +

Employment gaps are common and don't disqualify you from finding great opportunities. We can help by:

  • Providing strategies to address gaps during interviews
  • Formatting resume to emphasize skills over chronology
  • Identifying employers who value potential over perfect work history
  • Connecting you with opportunities that provide on-the-job training

Many of our employer partners understand that career paths aren't always linear and value diverse experiences.
