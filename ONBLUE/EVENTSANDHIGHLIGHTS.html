Expanding Opportunities. Strengthening Workforce Connections.

Stay informed about our latest hiring events, career fairs, and workforce development initiatives. We actively host and participate in events across Chicago, ensuring job seekers have access to real opportunities and employers can connect with ready-to-work talent.

Our events include:

Hiring Events & Job Fairs – Meet employers actively looking to hire and explore open roles across industries.

Career Readiness Workshops – Gain insights on resume writing, interview preparation, and workplace expectations.

Why Attend?

  • For Job Seekers: Access hiring managers, submit applications, and even interview on-site.
  • For Employers: Connect with motivated candidates and streamline recruitment efforts.
